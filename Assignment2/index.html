Happiness Unveiled: A Worldwide Happiness Analysis for 2016

Welcome to the World Happiness Report Visualization Portal! Explore the fascinating world of global happiness through a series of interactive graphs and charts based on the renowned World Happiness Report. This collection of visualizations delves deep into the factors influencing happiness levels across countrie s and regions, offering insights into well-being, economics, social support, and more.

Join me on a journey to uncover the secrets of happiness worldwide.

Mapping World Happiness Score by Country

Our chloropleth map provides a captivating visual representation of happiness scores across countries. Each nation is color-coded, with darker shades indicating higher happiness scores and lighter shades representing lower scores. Dive into this map to gain a quick overview of global happiness distribution, showcasing regions of prosperity and areas facing well-being challenges.

The slider can be used to filter countries based on minimum happiness score

This histogram zooms in on the top 10 and bottom 10 countries in terms of happiness scores. It offers a concise comparison of the happiest and least happy nations, allowing you to explore the factors contributing to their rankings. Discover what sets these countries apart on the happiness scale.

In case it wasn't clear from the map above, we can see that Denmark has the highest happiness score followed by Switzerland. The least happy country is Burundi with a lowest score of 2.905.

The checkbox can be used to filter top 10 and bottom 10 countries

Boxplot

Our boxplot provides a comprehensive view of happiness scores across different regions. Each box represents an individual region, showcasing the interquartile range of scores, the median, and the range of happiness levels within each region. Uncover the variations in happiness across different parts of the world and gain insights into regional well-being disparities.

Fourth Visualisation

The bubble plot illustrates the relationship between a country's GDP per capita and its happiness score. Each bubble represents a nation, with the bubble size indicating its population. Explore how economic prosperity influences happiness levels and discover intriguing outliers that challenge conventional wisdom.

The drop down menu below the graph allows you to choose the region you would like to see.

Fourth Visualisation

Our parallel coordinates plot offers a holistic view of the various factors contributing to a country's happiness score. Each line represents a nation, weaving through axes that represent attributes such as "Happiness Score" , "GDP per capita," "Family," "Life Expectancy," "Freedom," "Government Corruption," "Generosity," and "Dystopia Residual." Analyze how these attributes intersect to shape a nation's overall happiness.

Enter the name of a country you want to focus on in the search bar to view that country's data exclusively.
